Information about our page:

If you, like most people have loads of different ingredients

in your fridge but can't come up with anything to cook.

Simply enter your ingredients into our search field and we

will compare them with our database and show you all our

recepies containing those ingredients and suggest recepies

for your to cook. We will also provide you with recipies which

you are close to being able to make. This feature can be toggled

via our smart filtration system.
